Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you contact us, request a consultation, or subscribe to our newsletter. This may include:

  • Name and contact information
  • Company information and job title
  • Business requirements and technology needs
  • Communication preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide consulting services and support
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Send you relevant technology insights and updates
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy:

  • Trusted technology partners for service delivery
  • Legal requirements or court orders
  • Business transfers (mergers, acquisitions)

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
