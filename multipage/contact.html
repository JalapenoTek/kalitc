Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Kali Technology Consulting

What does Kali Technology Consulting do?

Kali Technology Consulting, LLC is a trusted MSP technology consultant providing access to over 200 leading technology suppliers. We deliver enterprise-grade telecom, cloud, cybersecurity, and IT solutions with preferential pricing and dedicated support.

Does it cost anything to get a consultation?

No. Our consultations, assessments, and quotes are completely free. We're compensated by the providers, so there's no cost to you for our services — and you often get better pricing than going direct.

How many providers do you work with?

We have access to over 200 leading technology providers across 8 solution categories including network, cloud, security, UC, contact center, managed services, mobility/IoT, and SD-WAN.

Are you vendor-neutral?

Yes. We recommend solutions based solely on what's best for your business. With 200+ providers to choose from, we have no incentive to push any single vendor — our goal is finding the perfect fit for your specific needs and budget.

What size companies do you work with?

We work with businesses of all sizes — from startups with 10 employees to enterprises with 10,000+. Our provider portfolio includes solutions scaled for every business size and budget.
