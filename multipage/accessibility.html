Accessibility Statement

Kali Technology Consulting, LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users.

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Kali Technology Consulting, LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Accessibility Standards

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Alternative text for images
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Clear and consistent navigation
  • Responsive design for all screen sizes
  • High contrast color combinations
  • Descriptive link text
  • Form labels and error messages
  • Skip to main content links

Assistive Technology Compatibility

Our website is designed to be compatible with assistive technologies, including:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Voice recognition software
  • Alternative input devices

Keyboard Navigation

Our website can be navigated using only a keyboard:

  • Tab key moves between interactive elements
  • Shift+Tab moves backward through elements
  • Enter and Space activate buttons and links
  • Escape key closes menus and modals
  • Arrow keys navigate within menus and lists

Visual Design

We've implemented visual accessibility features:

  • Sufficient color contrast ratios (4.5:1 or higher)
  • Resizable text without loss of functionality
  • Consistent layout and navigation
  • Clear focus indicators
  • Readable fonts and appropriate spacing

Accessibility Testing

We regularly test our website using:

  • Automated accessibility testing tools
  • Manual keyboard navigation testing
  • Screen reader testing
  • User testing with people with disabilities

Ongoing Efforts

We are committed to ongoing accessibility improvements through:

  • Regular accessibility audits
  • Staff training on accessibility best practices
  • Incorporating accessibility into our development process
  • Gathering feedback from users

Third-Party Content

While we strive to make our entire website accessible, some third-party content may not fully conform to accessibility standards. We are working with our partners to improve their accessibility.

Alternative Formats

If you need information in an alternative format, such as large print, Braille, or audio, please contact us and we will provide it in a format that meets your needs.

Formal Complaints

If you encounter an accessibility issue that we are unable to resolve to your satisfaction, you may file a formal complaint with:

  • U.S. Department of Justice, Civil Rights Division
  • Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC)
  • Your state's human rights agency

Future Improvements

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our website. Our roadmap includes:

  • Enhanced keyboard navigation
  • Improved screen reader compatibility
  • Additional language support
  • Advanced accessibility features

Statement Updates

This accessibility statement will be reviewed and updated periodically to reflect our ongoing commitment to accessibility and any changes in accessibility standards or regulations.
